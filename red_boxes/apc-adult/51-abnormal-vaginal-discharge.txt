Give urgent attention if any of:
• Recent delivery/TOP/miscarriage
• Pregnant or missed/overdue period
• Abnormal vaginal bleeding
• Temperature ≥ 38°C
• Pulse > 100
• BP < 90/60
• Abdominal mass
• Peritonitis (guarding,
rigidity, rebound)
Manage and refer urgently:
• If BP < 90/60, give sodium chloride 0.9% 500mL IV rapidly, repeat until systolic BP > 90. Continue 1L 6 hourly.
Stop if breathing worsens.
• Give ceftriaxone4 1g IV (avoid diluting with lidocaine 1%) and metronidazole3 400mg orally.
